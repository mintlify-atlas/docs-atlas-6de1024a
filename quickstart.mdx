## Getting Started

This guide will walk you through using MiTensión for the first time, from recording your first measurement to viewing your blood pressure history in the calendar.

<Note>
Make sure you have installed MiTensión on your device. See the [Installation Guide](/installation) if you haven't done so yet.
</Note>

## First Launch

When you launch MiTensión for the first time:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Grant Notification Permission">
    On Android 13 and higher, the app will request permission to send notifications.
    
    <Card>
      **Why does MiTensión need notification permission?**
      
      The app sends periodic reminders (every 2 hours) to help you remember to take your blood pressure measurements. This is completely optional.
    </Card>
    
    - Tap **Allow** to enable reminders
    - Tap **Don't allow** if you prefer to track measurements manually
    
    You can change this later in your device settings.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Understand the Main Screen">
    You'll see the **Measurement Screen** with:
    - **Title**: Shows the current period and measurement number (e.g., "Mañana - Medición 1/3")
    - **Systolic field**: "La Alta (sistólica)" - your systolic blood pressure
    - **Diastolic field**: "La Baja (diastólica)" - your diastolic blood pressure
    - **Two buttons**: "Guardar" (Save) and "Ver Calendario" (View Calendar)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Recording Your First Measurement

Let's record your first blood pressure measurement.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check the Current Period">
    The app automatically determines which period you're in based on the current time:
    
    - **Morning (Mañana)**: 12:01 AM - 12:30 PM
    - **Afternoon (Tarde)**: 12:31 PM - 7:00 PM
    - **Night (Noche)**: 7:01 PM - 12:00 AM
    
    The title at the top shows which period is active.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Systolic Value">
    1. Tap on the **"La Alta (sistólica)"** field (it shows "Pulsa para añadir")
    2. A dialog will appear with a numeric keyboard
    3. Enter your systolic value (e.g., 120)
    4. Tap **Confirmar** or press the Done button on the keyboard
    
    <Note>
    You can enter values up to 3 digits. The input only accepts numbers.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Diastolic Value">
    1. Tap on the **"La Baja (diastólica)"** field
    2. Enter your diastolic value (e.g., 80)
    3. Tap **Confirmar**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save the Measurement">
    1. Tap the **"Guardar"** button
    2. You'll see a success message: "¡Medición guardada con éxito!"
    3. The app will automatically clear the fields for your next measurement
    4. The title updates to show "Medición 2/3"
    
    <Note>
    Each measurement is automatically timestamped and stored in the local database.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Blood Pressure Classifications

MiTensión automatically classifies your measurements according to AHA guidelines:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Low (Baja)" icon="arrow-down" color="#0000FF">
    **Systolic < 90** or **Diastolic < 60**
    
    Color indicator: Blue
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Normal" icon="check" color="#008000">
    **Systolic < 120** and **Diastolic < 80**
    
    Color indicator: Green
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Elevated (Elevada)" icon="circle-exclamation" color="#FFA500">
    **Systolic 120-129** and **Diastolic < 80**
    
    Color indicator: Orange
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="High Stage 1 (Alta 1)" icon="triangle-exclamation" color="#FF0000">
    **Systolic 130-139** or **Diastolic 80-89**
    
    Color indicator: Red
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="High Stage 2 (Alta 2)" icon="exclamation" color="#DC143C">
    **Systolic ≥ 140** or **Diastolic ≥ 90**
    
    Color indicator: Crimson
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Crisis" icon="siren-on" color="#8B0000">
    **Systolic > 180** or **Diastolic > 120**
    
    Color indicator: Dark Red
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
If you get a "Crisis Hipertensiva" reading, seek immediate medical attention. This app is for tracking only and does not replace medical advice.
</Warning>

## Multiple Measurements Per Period

MiTensión allows you to record up to **3 measurements per period** to get a more accurate average.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Record Additional Measurements">
    After saving your first measurement:
    1. Wait a few minutes (recommended by medical guidelines)
    2. Enter your second measurement
    3. Tap **Guardar** again
    4. Repeat for the third measurement
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Period Complete">
    After recording 3 measurements for a period:
    - The title changes to "Ya has completado las mediciones de [Period]"
    - You'll see a message indicating when the next period starts
    - You can still access the calendar to view your measurements
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Next Period">
    When the next period begins:
    - The app automatically resets to "Medición 1/3"
    - You can start recording measurements for the new period
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
The app calculates the average of your measurements for each period automatically. These averages are displayed in the calendar view.
</Note>

## Viewing Your Calendar

The calendar provides a visual overview of all your measurements.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Calendar View">
    From the measurement screen, tap **"Ver Calendario"** button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate the Calendar">
    The calendar displays:
    - Current month and year at the top
    - Days of the week (L M X J V S D)
    - Each day shows three color bars representing morning, afternoon, and night
    
    **Navigation:**
    - Tap the **left arrow** to view previous months
    - Tap the **right arrow** to view future months
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Understand Color Indicators">
    Each day cell shows three horizontal color bars:
    
    ```
    [Morning] [Afternoon] [Night]
    ```
    
    - **Colored bar**: Measurements recorded for that period
    - **Transparent bar**: No measurements for that period
    - The color indicates the average blood pressure classification for that period
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Color Legend">
    To see what each color means:
    1. Tap the **three-dot menu** icon in the top-right corner
    2. Select **"Leyenda de colores"** (Color Legend)
    3. A dialog shows all classifications and their colors
    4. Tap **"Cerrar"** to close the legend
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Day Details">
    Tap on any day in the calendar to view detailed information about measurements for that specific day.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Scenarios

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What if I forgot to enter a value?">
    If you try to save without entering both systolic and diastolic values, you'll see an error:
    
    **"Ambos campos, Alta y Baja, son obligatorios."**
    
    Both fields are required. Simply enter the missing value and tap Save again.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I edit or delete a measurement?">
    Currently, the app focuses on recording new measurements. To maintain data integrity:
    - Recorded measurements cannot be edited directly through the UI
    - Make sure your values are correct before tapping "Guardar"
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens if I try to record more than 3 measurements?">
    The app will show an error message:
    
    **"Ya has completado los 3 registros para este período. El siguiente período empieza en [time]."**
    
    Wait for the next period to start recording more measurements.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do I add a measurement for a previous period?">
    Measurements are timestamped when saved and automatically assigned to the current period. The app is designed for real-time tracking:
    - Always record measurements as you take them
    - The timestamp cannot be manually adjusted
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do I need internet to use the app?">
    No! MiTensión works completely offline:
    - All measurements are stored locally on your device
    - No internet connection required
    - Your data never leaves your device
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Setting Up Reminders

MiTensión automatically schedules periodic reminders to help you maintain consistent tracking.

**Reminder Configuration:**
- **Frequency**: Every 2 hours
- **Technology**: WorkManager (Android's recommended task scheduler)
- **Battery Impact**: Minimal - WorkManager optimizes execution

**Reminder Notification:**
```
Title: "¡Es hora de registrar tu tensión!"
Message: "Aún te faltan X mediciones para completar el período de la [period]."
```

The notification shows how many measurements you still need for the current period.

<Note>
Reminders require notification permission. If you denied permission initially, you can enable it later in your device Settings > Apps > MiTensión > Permissions.
</Note>

## Multi-Language Support

MiTensión supports three languages based on your device settings:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Español (Default)">
    - La Alta (sistólica) / La Baja (diastólica)
    - Mañana / Tarde / Noche
    - Guardar / Ver Calendario
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="English">
    The app will display English labels if your device language is set to English.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Galego">
    The app will display Galician labels if your device language is set to Galician.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

To change the language:
1. Go to your device **Settings > System > Languages**
2. Change your system language
3. Reopen MiTensión
