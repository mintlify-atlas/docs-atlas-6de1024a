<Note>
MiTensión is a comprehensive blood pressure tracking application designed to help you monitor and manage your cardiovascular health with ease.
</Note>

## Key Features

MiTensión offers powerful features to help you track your blood pressure effectively:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Smart Measurements" icon="heart-pulse" href="/features/recording-measurements">
    Record up to 3 measurements per period (Morning, Afternoon, Night) with automatic validation
  </Card>
  <Card title="Calendar Heatmap" icon="calendar-days" href="/features/calendar-view">
    Visualize your blood pressure trends with color-coded daily indicators
  </Card>
  <Card title="Detailed Analytics" icon="chart-line" href="/features/day-details">
    View period-based groupings with blood pressure classification
  </Card>
  <Card title="Smart Reminders" icon="bell" href="/features/notifications">
    Never miss a measurement with WorkManager-powered notifications
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
